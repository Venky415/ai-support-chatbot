How can I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page.

How do I connect to VPN?
Install the VPN client and login using your company credentials.

My laptop is slow. What should I do?
Restart the laptop and close unused applications.

Printer is not working.
Check cable connection and reinstall printer drivers.

How do I configure email on Outlook?
Open Outlook → Add account → Enter company email credentials.
