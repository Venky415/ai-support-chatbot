# Success Metrics

## Primary Metrics

### Answer Relevance

Percentage of responses rated relevant to user query.

Target: >85%

---

### Hallucination Rate

Percentage of responses containing unsupported information.

Target: <10%

---

### First Response Accuracy

Percentage of queries correctly answered without rephrasing.

Target: >80%

---

### Response Latency

Time between query submission and response generation.

Target: <2 seconds

---

## Business Metrics

### Manual Support Reduction

Reduction in repetitive support workload.

Target: 25%

### Support Resolution Efficiency

Improvement in support team productivity.

Target: 20%
